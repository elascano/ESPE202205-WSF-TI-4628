Bikes Pogo's

MISSION

Bikes Pogo's is the reference entity in Spain for the promotion of the bicycle as a regular, safe and sustainable means of transport, which contributes to improving the quality of life of the population and a fairer social system.

Bikes Pogo's supports, represents and defends before public and private entities the rights and interests of people who use the bicycle as a means of transport or leisure, in order to implement measures, regulations, infrastructure and everything that promotes the culture of the bike.

ViSION

Bikes Pogo's is a proactive coordinator in the promotion of the bicycle, with recognition and influence, which represents the interests of cyclists at the state level and in each of the territories in which it is represented. It is an organization with management capacity, in which all its member groups actively participate, both in the organization and decision-making as well as in the preparation and development of the activities and services it offers. ConBici creates synergies and positive alliances with other entities, respecting their independence at all times.
