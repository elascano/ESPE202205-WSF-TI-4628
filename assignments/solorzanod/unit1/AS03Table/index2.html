Today, organisations of all types have become dependent on information technology. From this perspective, organisations need information systems that are secure, up-to-date, maintainable and adapted to each reality. On the other hand, and with the purpose of satisfying these needs, IT specialists are required to provide support in the selection of the most appropriate hardware and software products for each organisation.

In this sense, the degree in Information Technology Engineering includes in its academic training, the development of: web environments, mobile applications, intelligent systems and software in general; as well as the management of: databases, computer security, IT infrastructures, communication networks and telematic services. infrastructures, communication networks and telematic services.
