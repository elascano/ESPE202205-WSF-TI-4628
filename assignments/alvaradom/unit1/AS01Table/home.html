Universidad de las Fuerzas Armadas - ESPE

The University of the Armed Forces ESPE has its origin when on June 16, 1922 the "School of Engineers Officers" was created, by Decree of the President of the Republic, Dr. José Luis Tamayo, published in the Official Register 521; In view of the need to technify the commands in the engineering and artillery specialties, on October 22, 1936, during the Presidency of Federico Páez, the name was changed to "School of Artillery and Engineers". By expanding its academic curriculum and leveling it with other Ecuadorian universities, in 1948 it was called the Technical School of Engineers. Faced with the university crisis in the country and the needs of the Armed Forces, in 1972 General Guillermo Rodríguez Lara, President of the Republic, opened the doors for civilian students to enter.
