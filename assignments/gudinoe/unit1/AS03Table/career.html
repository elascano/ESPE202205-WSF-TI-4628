INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ENGINEERING

CAREER DESCRIPTION

Train Information Technology professionals, with solid scientific and technological knowledge in the areas of: technological infrastructure, computer networks, human-machine interaction, databases, web development; capable of planning, designing, implementing, implementing and maintaining secure IT solutions using international methodologies and standards; in order to satisfy the technological needs of businesses, government, health system, schools, organizations of all kinds and contribute to the reduction of the digital divide in the community; acting with a high sense of research and responsibility towards society.
