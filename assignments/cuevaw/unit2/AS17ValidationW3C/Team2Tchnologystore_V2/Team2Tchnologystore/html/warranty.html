Warranty Policies

  • The customer, whose name appears on the invoice, must present proof of purchase of the equipment and this guarantee certificate.
  • SUPER TECH agrees to repair during the warranty period those products that show damage during normal use, according to the instructions in the user manual and the purpose for which the equipment was designed.
  • The guarantee is not transferable.
  • The new SuperTech warranty does not cover damage due to misuse.

The guarantee will not be valid in the following circumstances:

  • Damage due to analysis, operation, maintenance, adjustment or any alteration or modification caused by unauthorized personnel.
  • Defect or damage caused by misuse of the product or in conditions other than its handling; that is, improper use intentionally or recklessly that affects the external appearance and operation of the equipment or accessory, for example: excessive external forces, falls, humidity, etc.
  • Damage to the equipment's antenna, unless it is caused by a manufacturing defect.
  • When the equipment or accessory does not have the labels or mechanical and electronic serial number, as well as its brand.
  • If the accessories are broken or were improperly used.
  • The expenses of installation, maintenance and explanation of the operation are not covered by any guarantee, therefore they are the entire responsibility of the consumer.
