About us

Car Rental is an auto rental service based in Quito, offering a range of daily car rentals. Make your trip to the beautiful city of Quito absolutely perfect by choosing one of our many rental options.


Personalized Attention

It doesn’t matter if you’re here for business, or for pleasure. We have a car that matches all your needs. We have minivan rentals for those family road trips and vacations, offering complete safety and functionality. We also have convertible car rentals for when you want to let loose and have fun with a group of close friends!

All our vehicles are in prime condition, with most of them being new. They are well-maintained, comfortable and come with no hidden charges. All our rates are upfront and you can get a quote from our website or speak to our customer representatives.
