IT Engineering



Information Technology engineering is made up of the set of resources necessary for information processing, as well as the administration or management of communications. Creates, designs, implements and manages computer systems related to Information and Communication Technologies, as well as computer networks, databases for decision-making and devices with embedded software.

What Information Technology Engineer do?
The Information Technology Engineer is a professional who focuses on the integration, implementation, operation and administration of computer systems using computer technologies as the main tool, as well as operating and managing communications and telecommunications.

Where do they work?
You can work in the Industry, in establishments where the administration of the goods marketed using technology is required, in companies dedicated to communications or telecommunications services or in government where it is required to develop computer systems for the management or administration of large volumes of information.

General Objective of the Career
Train professionals capable of integrating and managing information and communications technologies, which contribute to productivity and the achievement of the strategic objectives of organizations; characterized by being leaders, critical, competent, ethical and with a business vision, committed to sustainable development.
